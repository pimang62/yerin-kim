We introduce FiRe, a framework for fine-grained multimodal reasoning aimed at enhancing image generation. FiRe reasons over detailed visual and textual cues to better align generated images with complex, compositional prompts. By incorporating structured multimodal reasoning prior to synthesis, FiRe improves the fidelity and controllability of generated images compared to approaches that generate images without explicit fine-grained reasoning.
