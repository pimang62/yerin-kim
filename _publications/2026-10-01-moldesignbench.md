We present MolDesignBench, a benchmark designed to evaluate LLM-based agents on scenario-grounded molecular design. Unlike prior benchmarks that focus on isolated property prediction or unconstrained generation, MolDesignBench situates molecular design within realistic scenarios, requiring agents to reason about design objectives and constraints before proposing candidate molecules. Through this benchmark, we systematically assess the strengths and limitations of current LLM-based agents in practical molecular design settings.
